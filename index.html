## Background

I'm a CSIT student at NIST College, Banepa, currently in my final year. Most of what I know about software I picked up by building things that were slightly too ambitious for my skill level at the time — an astrology platform with live video calls, a multiplayer chess server, an incident-reporting app with maps and notifications. Each one forced me to learn the next piece: authentication, WebSockets, third-party APIs, deployment.

I like backend work the most — designing the data model, getting the API right, making sure two users' actions stay in sync — but I can carry a project through the frontend too when it needs one. I'm reasonably good at explaining technical decisions to people who didn't make them, which has helped more than I expected.
